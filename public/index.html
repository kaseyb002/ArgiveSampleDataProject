Division 10, Chapter 6

Air Pollution Regulations for the State of Missouri

Original Data (Google Sheets)

Rule Summary

This rule regulates activities that emit air pollutants. Any person or organization that emits one or more of the regulated pollutants* above a certain threshold will be subject to these regulations.

This chapter covers permits, emission limits, the cap and trade program, penalties, and other topics.

* Regulated pollutants: carbon monoxide, lead, nitro-gen dioxide, ozone, particle pollution, sulfur dioxide, hydrogen sulfide, and sulfuric acid
