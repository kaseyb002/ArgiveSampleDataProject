Missouri 10 CSR 10-6.065

Air Pollution Permits

Original Data (Google Sheets)

Rule Summary

Any business that emits regulated pollutants above a certain threshold must obtain a permit. The permit requires a filing fee of at least $500 and a detailed application form. Authorities can require a demonstration of compliance at any time.
